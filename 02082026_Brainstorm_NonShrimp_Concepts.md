# 02/08/2026 - Brainstorm: Non-Shrimp / Non-Food Variations

Pivoting the **Wok Controller** away from cooking entirely to explore its physical properties (heavy, circular, concave, handle).

## 1. "Panning for Gold" (Historical Simulation)
*   **Concept:** You are a 1849 Gold Rush prospector. The wok is your **panning dish**.
*   **Loop:** Scoop up virtual dirt (tilt forward), wash it in the river (gentle swirling motion), and shake out the debris (toss/shake) to reveal gold nuggets. Too much shaking loses the gold!
*   **Why GDC:** Perfectly mimics the real-world action. Very satisfying, low-stress, tactile loop. "The only controller that makes you rich."

## 2. "Satellite Hunter" (Sci-Fi Signal Decoding)
*   **Concept:** The wok is a **parabolic dish** receiver. You are listening for alien signals in deep space.
*   **Loop:** Hold the heavy wok steady to "catch" a signal. **Fine tilting** to tune the frequency (haptic feedback gets stronger). **Rotate** to adjust polarization.
*   **Why GDC:** Turns the wok's weight into a feature (heft = stability). Very immersive "SETI" experience.

## 3. "Gladiator's Buckler" (VR/Action Defense)
*   **Concept:** The wok is a **small round shield (buckler)**. You are in a gladiator arena.
*   **Loop:** Use the wok to **physically block** incoming arrows and sword strikes. **Punch forward** (thrust) to shield-bash enemies.
*   **Why GDC:** High physicality. Players feel like a warrior. The metal "CLANG" when you block (audio feedback) feels incredible.

## 4. "Battle Top Arena" (Beyblade Physics)
*   **Concept:** You control the **Arena Floor**, not the character. Two spinning tops are fighting in your bowl.
*   **Loop:** **Tilt** the wok to alter gravity, sliding your top into the enemy or dodging attacks. **Toss** to launch your top into the air for a "Death Dive" attack.
*   **Why GDC:** A "God Game" perspective. You physically hold the entire battlefield in your hands.

## 5. "Hazmat Hero" (Balance/Puzzle)
*   **Concept:** You are transporting **unstable radioactive sludge** through a bumpy facility.
*   **Loop:** The sludge is a fluid sim. You must walk/move while keeping the wok perfectly level. Any **sudden jerk** or **tilt > 15 degrees** causes a spill = meltdown.
*   **Why GDC:** Tension! The audience watches the player walking carefully like a waiter, trying not to spill the virtual glowing ooze.
