## CHANGELOG — Shrimp Fried Rice v0.2.0

**Date:** Feb 18, 2026 | **Build:** index.html | **Previous:** index.html (v0.1.0)

### ADDED — Stage System
- **Two-stage game loop** with narrative transition
- **Stage 1: "Survive the Wok"** — dodge obstacles, collect MSG crystals (5 needed), stay oiled
- **Stage 2: "Cook the Chef"** — chef hand reaches into wok, player SWATS to damage (4 HP to win)
- **Stage transition cutscene** — text sequence ("The MSG... it's changing you..." → "NOW YOU'RE THE CHEF")
- **Stage announcements** — title cards flash at start of each stage
- **Victory screen** with stats on chef defeat

### ADDED — Obstacles (Stage 1 & 2)
- **Spatula Sweep** — rotating line sweeps 180° across wok (telegraph: dashed arc warning 1.2s before)
- **Pepper Bombs** — warning crosshair → AoE damage zone with rotating flake particles
- **Oil Pops** — bubble → expanding ring. All three drain oil on hit; grounded only (airborne dodges)
- **Knockback** on spatula hits pushes shrimp perpendicular to sweep
- **Hit invulnerability** — 0.5s cooldown prevents multi-hits from single obstacle
- **Obstacle frequency scales** — slower in Stage 2 (focus shifts to chef)

### ADDED — Chef Enemy (Stage 2)
- **Chef AI state machine**: `idle → reaching → holding → recoiling`
- **Chef hand** rendered as arm (white sleeve) + skin-tone hand + chopsticks
- **Swat zone indicator** — pulsing dashed circle + "SHAKE!" prompt when holdable
- **Two damage methods**: SWAT (shake hard / tap — 1 HP) and RAM (airborne collision — 0.5 HP)
- **Chef HP bar** at top of screen
- **Hand color darkens** as HP drops (visual burn feedback)
- **Damage flash** (invulnerability blink) on chef after hit
- Chef **repositions** along rim between reaches

### ADDED — MSG Collectibles
- **MSG crystals** — white diamond shape with inner glow, rotating, labeled "MSG"
- **Ambient sparkle particles** on MSG items
- **Collection counter** in HUD (`0/5`)
- Collect while airborne (same as oil drops)
- **Triggers stage transition** on 5th MSG pickup

### ADDED — Procedural Audio Engine (Web Audio API)
- **6 procedural sounds** — no audio files needed:
  - `slap` — noise burst + low-pass filter (swat feedback)
  - `collect` — sine chirp sweep (oil/MSG pickup)
  - `whoosh` — bandpass filtered noise (toss)
  - `pop` — descending sine (oil pop / pepper)
  - `hit` — sharp noise crack (damage taken)
  - `victory` — 4-note ascending triangle arpeggio (C5-E5-G5-C6)
- Lazy `AudioContext` init on first user gesture (browser policy compliant)

### ADDED — Juice / Game Feel
- **Screen shake** system with intensity decay (`0.85` per frame)
- **Screen flash** (white) on swat
- **Red vignette** hit flash on damage
- **Slow-motion** (timeScale → 0.15 for 250ms) on successful swat
- **Haptic feedback** — `navigator.vibrate()` on swat (pattern: 100ms-30ms-60ms) and hit (50ms)
- **MSG aura** — golden radial glow on shrimp in Stage 2
- **Text popups** — "SWAT!", "RAM!", "+OIL", "MSG 3/5" float up from impact point

### CHANGED — Game Design
- Oil drain rebalanced: base `0.4` (was `0.5`), movement coefficient `0.015` (was `0.02`)
- Air oil drain reduced to 40% of base (was 50%) — reward for jumping
- Burn rate growth `55` (was `60`) — slightly more forgiving
- Shrimp body color shifts to golden in Stage 2 (MSG-powered)
- HUD dynamically switches labels between stages (MSG counter → Chef HP)

### FIXED — Bugs from v0.1.0
1. **Restart on tap** — game over / victory screens now respond to touch AND click (not just Space)
2. **Oil item memory leak** — items now spliced immediately on collection instead of `setTimeout` race
3. **No dedicated touch handler** — added `touchstart` on canvas with `passive: false` and `preventDefault()`
4. **Particle array unbounded** — unchanged but now capped to shorter lifetimes
5. **Resize mid-game** — `ctx.setTransform()` used instead of cumulative `ctx.scale()` calls
6. **Burn pulse persists** — `document.body.classList.remove('burn-pulse')` on reset/stage change
7. **WASD keys not working** — added `KeyW/A/S/D` alongside arrow keys

### TECHNICAL
- **Single HTML file** — no external dependencies, itch.io WebGL-ready (just zip `build/`)
- **Config object** (`CFG`) centralizes all tunable constants
- **State machine** architecture (`STAGE.TITLE → S1 → TRANSITION → S2 → VICTORY/GAMEOVER`)
- **Point-to-segment distance** function for precise spatula collision
- **Hardware-ready**: `DeviceMotionEvent` threshold at `22` for swat vs `15` for toss — maps directly to future piezo sensor (high impact = swat, medium = toss)
- Comment marking where Arduino/ESP32 WebSerial input would replace browser sensor APIs
